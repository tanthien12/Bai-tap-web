A smile is a facial expression expression formed primarily by flexing the muscles at the sides of the mouth. It is
believed it takes about 80 muscles to smile. Some miles include a contraction of the muscle at the comer of the eyes,
an action known a "Duchenne smile". Smiles performed without the eye contraction may be perceived as insincere.
